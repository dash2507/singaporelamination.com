Our Products

Top Coat

Polyester Clear

Polyester Clear is a Transparent polyester. It offers a high level quality transparency with good leveling parameters, excellent environmental and climate resistance. It never fades of even in long term. Its ultra clarity properties give excellent glass like sheen on any veneer or figure. It is our top most selling product.

Polyester Base White

Polyester Base White is a pigmented polyester, which gives Natural white shade ro your interiors. It has excellent physucal and chemical resistance. It never turns out yellowish even in long term. For natural white look it is highly recommended product.

Polyester Extra White

Polyester Extra White is a pigmented polyester, which gives Ultra white look to your interiors & furniture. It has excellent physical and chemical resistance. It never turns out yellowish even in long term. For vibrant white look always use Polyester Extra White.

Polyester Colour

Polyester Colour is a customized pigmented polyester, it is handcrafted as per the choice of your shade to give your interiors colourful looks. Our colour polyesters are prepared with utmost care to match your desired shade. Singapore Lamination has its custom made shade card that offers wide range of shades for your choice, apart from this we make shades from any other shade cards available in the market of any shadr of your choice.

Base Coat

Polyester Clear Sealer/White Filler

SINGAPORE FILLER/SEALER is used to create thick base for your desired top coats. It is a multipurpose product, can be used as a base coat for DUCO, POLY-URATHENE (PU) or POLYESTER TOP COATS. It comes in a four pack system, comprising of BASE, THINNER, ACCELERATOR & HARDENER. It can be applied by spray or putty blade as preferred. It gives the advantage of more thickness in the base with the coverage up to 30 sqft / kg.

Clear Epoxy Resin

It is CLEAR EPOXY also popularly known as ISOLANT/INSULATOR/PRIMER amongst the Interior Designers, Architects and Contractors. It is used as base coat before the application of Polyester top coats. It is highly recommended and must to be applied as it acts as excellent bonding agent between the desired surface of wood and the polyester top coats. It also eliminates the moisture and oil content present in ply, veneers and other wood surfaces. SINGAPORE CLEAR EPOXY is specially developed epoxy for the base coats on the desired surfaces. It is easy to apply and very quick in drying with the pot life of 15-20 mins. It has excellent physical resistance and is a non-yellowing product. It comes in a two pack system with base & hardener and should be mixed in the ratio of 1:1

SINGAPORE AAA ZINC PASTE

White Zinc Texture Paste is an oil based paste to be mixed along with Solvent Based Lustre Paint / Enamel Paint to create texture effect on walls and ceilings. It is more often used to create bubble texture on the surface like wall and ceiling.Bubble finish texture gives a very simple yet elegant look thus transforming your regular space into your royal space.This product gives excellent long lasting life of 10-15 years if the paint is applied properly and the substrate underneath is defect free.This product is water resistant and natural anti-microbial without adding any special synthetic additives.Once completely dried this product gives excellent hardness and washability.

Polyester Putty

It is universal 2 component polyester putty available in clear and white variant. It is primarily used to fill cracks, gaps, dents, joints and to level irregularities in the surfaces of wood. It is smooth & simple to apply. It is to be applied with putty blade. It can be applied in layers with the desired thickness. It is very easy to sand. It has a pot life of 20 mins. It gives the covering upto 50 sqft / kg.
